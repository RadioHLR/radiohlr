About Radio HLR

Welcome to Radio HLR, your dedicated community radio station! We are passionate about bringing you a diverse range of music, local news, engaging discussions and entertaining shows, all tailored to reflect the vibrant spirit of our community.

Our mission is to keep us all connected and entertain us. Whether you're looking for the latest community updates or a great soundtrack to your day, Radio HLR is here for you. We pride ourselves on being a truly local station, run by volunteers who are committed to making a positive impact.

Thank you for tuning in and being a part of the Radio HLR family. Your support helps us continue to grow and serve our listeners.
